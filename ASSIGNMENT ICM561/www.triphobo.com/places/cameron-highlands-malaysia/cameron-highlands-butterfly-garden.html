A place which is rich in tropical flora and fauna is bound to have a butterfly garden. Established in year 1990, the butterfly garden in Cameron offers a colourful delight to the visitors owing to its various butterflies. They flutter and fly past you and thus offer you a lifetime experience. The park has been built to restore the biodiversity of flora in Malaysia in its own way. For children as well as schoolers, this is an ideal place to have fun as well as learn.
